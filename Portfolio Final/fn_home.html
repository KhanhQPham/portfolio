Our Promise To You

We are the fitness club that makes fitness totally accessible to every BODY. We promise to help people from all walks of life achieve their individual health and fitness goals -- no matter their shape, size, age, experience level, schedule or budget. We will promote to our members, the community and the world that every BODY should be respected, celebrated and not judged.

Accessible

We make fitness totally accessible to everybody and every BODY type. We are the only St. Louis, employee- owned fitness club chain with more locations than any of our competitors in the area. We are where people work and live, offering the flexibility to visit the location(s) that work best for them. We can fit every schedule, every budget and have the amenities and classes to accommodate every workout style.

Committed

For over 25 years, Club Fitness has been committed to the success of every BODY we serve. We provide the most services, amenities, equipment and locations at the best value available in the region. We are progressive and forward-thinking, constantly adapting to the evolving health and fitness needs of our members. We have it all, ultimately making us the last club they'll ever need to join.
